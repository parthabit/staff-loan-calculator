How EMI Is Calculated

This loan calculator uses a structured repayment method where the loan amount and interest are calculated separately to give clear visibility of repayment.

Key Terms

  • P – Principal loan amount
  • N – Total loan tenure (in months)
  • R – Monthly interest rate

Monthly Interest Rate Formula:

R = Annual Interest Rate ÷ 12 ÷ 100

Repayment Structure

• Principal-only EMIs: The principal amount is divided equally over the selected number of months.

• Interest-only EMIs: Interest is calculated monthly on the original loan amount and remains fixed.

Example

Loan Amount = ₹10,00,000
Annual Interest Rate = 7.2%
Tenure = 120 months

Monthly Interest Rate:
7.2 ÷ 12 ÷ 100 = 0.006

Monthly Interest = ₹6,000
Total Interest = ₹1,80,000
Total Repayment = ₹11,80,000

This calculator helps avoid manual calculations and provides a clear breakup of principal and interest for easy understanding.
